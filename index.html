Hi, I'm Yasmeen Kamal

        

          I am a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo currently living in Canada. Originally from the Middle East, I’ve spent my life moving between countries, which has shaped my perspective and inspired my love for design. I really enjoy designing logos and rebranding, finding joy in transforming concepts into visuals that truly capture a brand’s identity. My work is rooted in connecting people. My goal is to merge aesthetics with functionality to create meaningful products.         

      
           
    
      

My Projects

      
                 
          

Minute Maid Redesign

          

            Reposition Minute Maid as the ultimate drink for young athletes and active children, bringing the brand closer to sports culture and school-time moments. The focus is on making Minute Maid the go-to refreshment that fits seamlessly into the routines of kids at school, on the court, and in between practice sessions. The new tagline, "A Minute is All You Need," captures the essence of the quick energy boost and hydration that young athletes crave during their busy, active lives.           

        
      
      
                                     
          

Documentary

          

            Click the image to see a documentary I directed. Positioned as a student-friendly haven, the cafe offers deals and discounts that make it a budget-friendly choice for those balancing academic life with finances. But it’s not just affordability that makes it special; it’s the community spirit and atmosphere that makes it so inviting. We hope to share how a simple cafe has become a gathering place, a home away from home, and a space where stories are shared, friendships are made, and memories are created.
